#NUMBERS & MATH
* `+` plus
* `-` minus
* `/` slash
* `*` asterisk
* `%` percent
* `<` less-than
* `>` greater-than
* `<=` less-than-equal
* `>=` greater-than-equal

How does % work?
Another way to say it is, "X divided by Y with J remaining." For example, "100 divided by 16 with 4 remaining." The result of % is the J part, or the remaining part.
